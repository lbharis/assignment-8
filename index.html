BREAKING NEWS: PINE WOOD

The Pinewood Garden Club was honored last night for its successful "Green Alley" initiative, which transformed a neglected downtown alleyway into a vibrant community space. The club received the "Civic Pride Award" from the city council during a public ceremony.

The project, which began in spring, saw volunteers from the club and local businesses work together to install planter boxes, vertical gardens, and solar-powered lighting. "This project was a true labor of love for everyone involved," said club president Maria Rodriguez. "We wanted to prove that even a small space can make a big difference in our community".
